Meat Lasagna

Timing & Heat

Cooking Time: 45 minutes • Oven: 350°F

Parmesan

Grated and stronger cheese

Lasagna Noodles

Wide pasta sheets for layering.

Ricotta Cheese

Creamy and rich texture.

Mozzarella

Melty topping cheese.

Ground Meat

Turkey or beef used in the sauce.

Marinara Sauce

Slow-simmered tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 lb Ricotta Cheese
  • 8 oz Mozzarella (sliced)
  • 1 cup Parmesan (grated)
Marinara Sauce
  • 1 medium onion, minced
  • 2–8 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb ground turkey or beef
  • 1 lb crushed Italian tomatoes
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Pinch of basil
  • 1 bay leaf

Instructions – Assembly

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions (8–12 minutes).
  2. Layer ingredients in a shallow pan.

Noodles → Ricotta → Mozzarella → Marinara → Parmesan

Instructions – Sauce

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add meat and cook until browned.
  3. Add remaining ingredients.
  4. Simmer for 1½ hours.
