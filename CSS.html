Recipe "Meat Lasagna"

Timing and Heat

Cooking Time: 45min - Preheat oven to 350*

Ingredients

  • 8oz Lasagna Noodles
  • 1LB Ricotta Cheese
  • 8oz Mozzarella Sliced
  • 1 Cup Parmesan Grated

Instructions 1

  1. Cook noodles for 25 minutes
  2. Arrange in shallow pan making 3 layers

Noodles, Ricotta, Mozzarella, Marinara, Parmesan

Marinara Sauce

  • 1 Medium Onion Minced
  • 2–8 Garlic Cloves, Minced
  • 2T Olive Oil
  • 1LB Ground Turkey or Beef
  • 1LB Crushed Italian Tomatoes
  • 2 Cups Water
  • 1T Salt
  • 1/8T Cayenne Pepper
  • 1T Sugar
  • Pinch Basil
  • 1 Bay Leaf

Instructions 2

  1. Brown onion and garlic in oil
  2. Add meat until browned
  3. Add remaining ingredients
  4. Simmer 1½ hours
