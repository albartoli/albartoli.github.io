Recipe "Meat Lasagna"

Timing and Heat

Cooking Time: 45min - Preheat oven to 350*

Ingredients

  • 8oz Lasagna Noodles
  • 1LB Ricotta Cheese
  • 8oz Mozzarella Sliced
  • 1 Cup Parmesan Grated

Instructions 1

  1. Cook Noodles For 25 min
  2. Arrange in Shallow Pan Making 3 Layers of

Noodles, Ricotta, Mozzarella, Marinara, Parmesan Cheese

Marinara Sauce

  • 1 Medium Onion Minced
  • 2-8 Garlic Cloves, Minced
  • 2T Olive Oil
  • 1LB Ground Turkey or Beef
  • 1LB Italian Tomatoes [Crushed]
  • 2 Cups Water
  • 1T Salt
  • 1/8T Cayenne Pepper
  • 1T Sugar
  • Pinch Basil
  • 1 Bay Leaf

Instructions 2

  1. Brown Onion and Garlic Oil in Saucepan
  2. Add Meat Until Browned - Stir with fork
  3. Add Remaining Meat
  4. Simmer 1 and 1/2 hours
